Valery Mihaylov

Front-end developer & UI/UX Designer

I work as Frontend Developer and UX/UI Designer from 9 years, for this time I developed many creative websites and apps (from design and collaboration with clients trough development and final release).

I know very well WordPress, Prestashop, FUEL and other CMS platforms. Last few years I am using Angular, Bootstrap, SASS and other modern frontend technologies. I love to learn and try new things and that is why I welcome every challenging project with a smile. On my last project I am trying TailwindCSS and Contentful - headless CMS.

Love clear and not obtrusive designs with pixel-perfect quality and animations. I was using Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator, but now I prefer Adobe Xd and Figma.

In my spare time I like to read, DJing and hangout with friends, but mostly to travel around the world with my wife. I will relocate to Barcelona, Spain in June and I can't wait for my next adventure!
